My Roadmap

How I'm planning on taking myself from complete ignorance to employment level

Some months ago I decided that the working opportunities that the world is offering were too good to pass, and set my mind into training myself to become a programmer.
Hence, I started two different but very complementary paths: the freeCodeCamp responsive web design certification and the CoderHouse frontend web development course.

After I complete this part of my education i'll continue studyng computer science through the Harvard University CS50 courses.

After that... The sky is the limit.
